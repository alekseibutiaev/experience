This project template is the basis of an expansion board for the NUCLEO-32 Project not includes a edge PCB, the size of PCB can by custom and connect to the NUCLEO-32 PCB through standard connector of board. Pinout matched with board MB1180.


Supported boards:


NUCLEO-F031K6

NUCLEO-F042K6

NUCLEO-F301K8

NUCLEO-F303K8

NUCLEO-L011K4

NUCLEO-L031K6

NUCLEO-L412KB

NUCLEO-L432KC
